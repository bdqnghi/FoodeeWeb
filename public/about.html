About Us

Foodee is a consumer mobile app that focuses only on restaurant and dish reviews for the American market. Foodee is a fun and informative platform where foodies can easily select what and where to eat; create memories and share their dining experiences through photos and videos. Both foodies and restaurants are offered the opportunity to showcase their best dining experiences and dishes. As the foodie community becomes bigger and more sophisticated, its members do need a centralized platform where they can easily and effectively communicate with and learn from each other. What makes Foodee unique is that we provide users the opportunity to be able to rate and review not only restaurants but also their specific dishes. With Foodee, dining will be made more fun and easy
